🏥 Care Point
AI-Powered Hospital Operational Intelligence Platform

From Reactive Dashboards to Predictive Operational Control

🚨 The Problem

Modern hospitals generate massive operational data — admissions, bed occupancy, doctor shifts, wait times.

Yet most systems:

Only show static reports

React after overload happens

Provide no short-term operational forecasting

Lack structured administrative intelligence

Hospital administrators are forced to make decisions without predictive visibility.

💡 Our Solution

Care Point is an AI-powered administrative analytics dashboard designed to:

Detect rising admission trends

Forecast ICU overload risk (48–72 hours ahead)

Highlight departmental bottlenecks

Monitor workforce imbalance

Provide structured operational alerts

Instead of just showing data, Care Point transforms it into actionable foresight.
